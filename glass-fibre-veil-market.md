<p><strong>The Glass Fibre Veil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Netting Process</li><li>Dry Netting Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass fibre veil market includes two primary types based on manufacturing processes: the wet netting process and the dry netting process. The wet netting process involves the application of a resin while the glass fibres are collected, enhancing inter-fibre bonding and resulting in stronger products. In contrast, the dry netting process involves assembling the fibres without resin, typically leading to lighter, more flexible materials. Both methods cater to different applications in industries like construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
