<p><strong>The Portable Doppler Ultrasound Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Directional Doppler</li><li>Non-Directional Doppler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable Doppler ultrasound systems market is divided into two main types: directional and non-directional Doppler systems. Directional Doppler systems are designed to measure blood flow direction and velocity, enhancing diagnostic accuracy for cardiovascular conditions. In contrast, non-directional Doppler systems provide basic information about flow but lack directional capabilities, making them simpler and often more affordable. Both types serve critical roles in medical diagnostics, particularly in remote or point-of-care settings where portability and ease of use are essential.</p></p>
