<p><ul><li>Chop Saw</li><li>Circular Saw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal cutting saw market primarily includes chop saws and circular saws, each serving distinct purposes. Chop saws, typically used for straight cuts, utilize a rotating abrasive wheel, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications in metal fabrication. Circular saws, on the other hand, employ toothed blades for versatile cutting, allowing for both straight and angled cuts on various metal types. Both tools are crucial in industries such as manufacturing, construction, and metalworking, facilitating efficient and precise metal processing tasks.</p></p>
