<p><p>The residential humidifier market includes several types, notably vapor type and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers function by heating water to produce steam, quickly increasing humidity levels and effectively sanitizing the air. On the other hand, water spray humidifiers use a mechanical process, such as a fan or ultrasonic technology, to disperse fine mist droplets. Each type caters to different user preferences and needs, enhancing indoor air quality and comfort in residential environments.</p></p>
