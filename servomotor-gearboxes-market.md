<p><p>The servomotor gearboxes market consists of various types that enhance motor efficiency and performance. Linear servo gearboxes convert rotational motion into linear motion, making them ideal for applications requiring precision and smooth movement, such as robotics and automation. Right angle servo gearboxes, on the other hand, change the direction of rotational motion by 90 degrees, suitable for compact spaces and complex machine designs. Both types facilitate precise control in diverse industries, including manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
